How to Get Involved

As the 7+H Schedule approaches sooner and sooner, only you can make a difference. Here, we have some ways you can do that.

Take Our Survey

We have a survey available here which you can take. Whether you write an essay or a sentence, we appreciate all answers. We plan on presenting our results at the town meeting this coming May 7th.

Contact Town Officials

Relaying what you think about the schedule to your officials is the best way to let them know what you think. Have your voice heard!

Volunteer for Us

We are always looking for passionate peers who want to help make a change at AHS. We understand AHS students are very busy, so don’t sweat it! We ask for minimal time involvement, and it’s all up to you whether you want to commit. Email us at info@no7h.com for more details.

Spread the News!

Tell your friends, parents, neighbors, and dogs about this! We want to make a difference, and just mentioning it to someone else makes a world of a difference. Thank you for making AHS a brighter place.
